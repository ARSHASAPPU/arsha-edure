The story behind the FourtyFive


In December 2021, we took over the former Trattoria al Solito Posto. We had many visions and ideas in our heads and with a large portion of creativity we were able to realize them step by step in the renovation of FortyFive. The new industrial chic in Solothurn. We were actively supported by family and friends. The entire renovation was carried out with combined forces and many hours of manual work over months. That is why we are particularly proud of the result of our heart project.
